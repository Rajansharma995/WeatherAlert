Alerts & Services

WeatherAlert uses a simple alert model based on temperature, rain, storms and wind. This page explains how alerts are represented.

Sunny / Heat Alerts

When the temperature is very high, WeatherAlert can show a heat warning. This reminds users to stay hydrated, avoid long periods in direct sunlight and check on vulnerable people.

Rain & Storm Alerts

For rainy or stormy conditions, WeatherAlert highlights that roads and footpaths may be slippery and that users should carry umbrellas or delay travel when possible.

Cold & Snow Alerts

In regions that experience very cold or snowy weather, alerts remind users to dress warmly, consider ice on roads and plan extra travel time.

How Live Alerts Work

On the home page, when a user searches for a city, WeatherAlert checks the live API response for thunderstorm codes, heavy rain, strong winds and high temperatures. If any rules are met, a dynamic alert card is shown below the weather result.

Prototype Only

This website is a prototype for learning purposes only. For real severe weather emergencies, users should always follow information from official government and emergency services websites.
